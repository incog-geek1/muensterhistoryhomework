Community Guidelines Back to Home

This is a list of guidelines you should follow if you like keeping your account.
Just to clarify, these rules apply to ALL content sent by you, including images.
NOTE: This version of the community guidelines will be replaced with this new one starting April 2nd.

Be kind and welcoming

Discrimination, bullying, harassment, etc. will not be tolerated here. Also, no depictions of Hitler, Stalin, etc. unless in an educational/negative way.

No spamming or malware

This rule covers spam and unwanted content of all kinds, as well as malware and adware.

Keep it SFW

This is meant to be used at school, and the userbase is mostly under 18. Please keep things clean here. This includes links.

Use common sense

Don't be a dumbass.

Moderate your swearing

You can swear, just try to keep it somewhat clean. Slurs will still get you banned.

No loopholes

Breaking this rule will get you banned with no appeal.

No impersonation

Breaking this rule will also get you banned with no appeal.

Do not give out personal information

Things that count as personal information include (but are not limited to): Your location (anything more specific than the state, this includes the schools you go to and the sports teams you're a part of), last name, phone number, email.

Punishments are at the moderator handling the situation's discretion.
